My Mission

I, Darius Kipruto, created My Kenya Report to give every Kenyan a voice in accountability.

My mission is to empower citizens to report issues, promote transparency, and hold institutions accountable. By building this platform, I aim to strengthen democracy and improve service delivery across all 47 counties.
